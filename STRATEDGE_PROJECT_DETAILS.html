Stratedge: Professional AI-Powered Trading Journal

Executive Summary: Stratedge is a state-of-the-art dual-market trading journal designed for professional traders. It bridges the gap between raw data and psychological discipline using AI-powered automation and institutional-grade analytics.

1. Project Vision & Market Importance

In modern finance, the biggest edge is data. Stratedge was built to solve the most common reason for trader failure: Emotional Bias. By turning every trade into a set of objective data points, we allow traders to see their "mirror image" in the markets.

  • Data-Driven Discipline: Highlights emotional flaws like revenge trading before they wipe out an account.
  • Multi-Market Efficiency: The only platform designed for seamless switching between Global Forex and Indian Equity/F&O.
  • Automated Workflow: 90% reduction in manual data entry using advanced OCR and AI extraction.

2. Feature Breakdown

🌍 Dual-Market Infrastructure

Architected as two specialized workspaces in one platform. Forex handles global currency pairs with USD/Pips, while the Indian Market handles NSE/BSE stocks and F&O with INR (₹) and specific tax calculations (STT, GST).

Usage: Traders switch instantly via the toggle. All analytics, currencies, and themes update dynamically.

🤖 AI-Extraction Engine

Leverages Google Vision AI and deep-learning parsers to read trade data from broker screenshots. It automatically identifies symbols, prices, lot sizes, and P&L.

Usage: Drag a screenshot from MetaTrader, Shoonya, or Kotak Neo, and the journal fills itself.

📊 Institutional Dashboard

Premium glassmorphism UI offering a high-performance "Control Center" feel. Features live equity curves, win/loss heatmaps, and strategy league tables.
