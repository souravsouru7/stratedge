# Stratedge: Professional AI-Powered Trading Journal & Analytics Platform

## 1. Project Vision & Market Importance
In the modern financial landscape, the difference between a successful trader and a failing one is often **discipline** and **data**. **Stratedge** was built to solve the "Journaling Gap"—the tedious process of manually recording trades and the lack of deep, actionable insights from that data.

### Why Stratedge is a Game-Changer:
- **Psychological Edge**: Most traders lose because of "tilt," revenge trading, or lack of a plan. Stratedge uses data to highlight these behavioral flaws.
- **Unified Market Access**: It is one of the few platforms that provides a seamless, high-end environment for both **Global Forex** and **Indian Equity/F&O** markets.
- **Workflow Automation**: By using advanced OCR (Optical Character Recognition) and AI, we reduce the time spent on manual entry by 90%, allowing traders to focus on analysis rather than data entry.

---

## 2. Comprehensive Feature Breakdown

### 🌍 Dual-Market Infrastructure
Stratedge is architected as two complete trading journals in one.
- **Forex Workspace**: Optimized for 24/5 global currency trading with USD ($) as the base currency and standard pip-based calculations.
- **Indian Market Workspace**: Custom-built for NSE/BSE trading, featuring INR (₹) formatting, support for Nifty/Bank Nifty options (CE/PE), and specific tax/brokerage calculations (STT, GST, etc.).
- **Usage**: A premium **Market Switcher** in the UI allows instant workspace switching. Data, themes, and settings are fully isolated between markets.

### 🤖 AI-Extraction Engine (The "Magic" Link)
- **High-Accuracy OCR**: Uses Google Vision AI (for Indian markets) and Tesseract to read trade data directly from mobile screenshots or desktop snips.
- **AI Parsing**: Our backend doesn't just "see" text; it "understands" it. It identifies the ticker, buy/sell type, entry price, stop-loss, and profit from raw text.
- **Usage**: Simply drag and drop a screenshot from MetaTrader, Shoonya, or any broker. The system populates the trade form automatically for your review.

### 📊 Advanced Interactive Dashboard
- **Glassmorphism Design**: A state-of-the-art UI that provides a premium, "hedge-fund" feel.
- **Real-time Stats**: Instant updates on Win Rate, Total Profit/Loss, and Net Profit after commissions.
- **Dynamic Charts**: Interactive equity curves and P&L breakdowns using high-performance charting libraries.

## 3. The Analytics Engine: A Deep Dive into Your "Edge"

Stratedge provided institutional-level metrics that help traders understand exactly where their money is being made or lost.

### A. Core Performance Metrics
| Metric | What it is | Why it's Important & How to Use It |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Profit Factor** | Ratio of Gross Profit / Gross Loss. | **The "Efficiency" Gauge**. A value > 1.5 is excellent. If it's < 1.0, your strategy is losing money even if you have a high win rate. |
| **Expectancy** | The average profit per trade. | **The "Mathematical Edge"**. This tells you what you can expect to win *on average* over 100 trades. Use this to set realistic income goals. |
| **Win Rate** | % of successful trades. | **Psychological Context**. A 40% win rate can be highly profitable if your Reward:Risk is high. This helps you stay calm during a 6-trade losing streak. |
| **Recovery Factor** | Profit / Max Drawdown. | **The "Resilience" Metric**. Shows how quickly you bounce back from losses. High recovery factor means low-stress growth. |

### B. Behavioral Analytics (Discipline Engine)
*This is the heart of Stratedge's value proposition for high-net-worth clients.*

#### 1. Rule Adherence & Quality Score
- **Concept**: Every trade is scored (0-100) based on how well you followed your pre-defined rules (e.g., "Wait for 15m candle close").
- **Usage**: A trader might be "Green" (profitable) but have a low Quality Score. This warns them that their profit was "luck" and they are at risk of a future blow-up.

#### 2. Entry Basis Analysis (Plan vs. Emotion)
- **Concept**: Tags trades as **Planned**, **Emotion**, or **Impulsive**.
- **Usage**: Visualize how much money you lose to "Impulsive" trades. This provides the "Financial Proof" a trader needs to stop overtrading.

#### 3. Revenge Trading & Tilt Detectors
- **Concept**: Algorithms that detect if you are increasing position size after a loss or taking too many trades in a short window.
- **Usage**: The system "red-flags" these behaviors in the weekly review. Seeing "REVENGE TRADE" on a dashboard is a powerful psychological deterrent.

### C. Strategic & Temporal Context
- **Session Edge**: Analyzes performance by Asia, London, and NY sessions.
  - *Usage*: You might discover you have a 70% win rate in London but lose everything in NY. The platform tells you: "Only trade London."
- **Asset Performance**: Rank your best-performing pairs (e.g., EURUSD vs. BANKNIFTY).
  - *Usage*: Stop trading instruments that "don't suit your personality." Maximize capital on your Winners.
- **Time Analysis (Day/Hour)**: Heatmaps of profit by day of week and hour of day.
  - *Usage*: Identify "Danger Zones." Many traders find they are most profitable on Tuesdays and lose most on Fridays.

## 4. Technology & Infrastructure

For the client, the "engine under the hood" ensures speed, security, and reliability.

- **Frontend**: Built with **Next.js & React**, providing a lightning-fast Single Page Application (SPA) experience.
- **Backend Architecture**: Scalable **Node.js** API using **Redis Caching** for instant dashboard loading even with thousands of trades.
- **Data Security**: Secure JWT authentication and industry-standard encryption for financial data.
- **Cloud Infrastructure**: Hosted on **AWS EC2** with a professional **CI/CD pipeline** (GitHub Actions). This means the project is "Production Ready" and can handle high traffic.
- **PWA Ready**: The platform can be "installed" on mobile or desktop, providing a native app experience without the app store friction.
